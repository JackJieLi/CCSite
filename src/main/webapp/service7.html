HSBC 微信银行解决方案

WeChat Banking

In local retail banking market, the digital innovation has been the hottest word in the past 20 years. With the soaring up of intelligent phone, ‘banking in your hand’ (mobile banking) has taken quite a proportion of the market share from ‘banking on your fingertips’ (internet banking on PC). And the emerging of WeChat banking has even pushed the line forward, -- banks are able to reach out further to their customers and to stick more closely to their daily life.

Most of local foreign banks have launched their WeChat banking with fairly good market feedback. To firm our market position, especially in retail market with the launch of credit card, we need to build up WeChat Banking as one of our digital pillars.

HSBC业务线人脸识别能力解决方案

应用场景：

HSBC员工之间要组织一个活动，但是由于员工数量众多，大多数员工之间并不认识，希望能够促进员工间的交流。

解决方案：

设计开发了遇见APP产品，员工只要安装在手机端且注册后。在员工活动时，对于不认识的人，只要需要用摄像头扫一扫，即可识别对方信息（包括姓名、爱好等等），方便员工之间相互了解交流。
